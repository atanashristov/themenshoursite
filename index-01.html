Master Barber "Ty"

Ty, the owner and operator of The Men's Hour, is a master barber with over twenty five years in men's grooming. His focus is on no-rush quality services for his clients while understanding classic and modern styles.

Come and relax today at The Men's Hour. Let Ty refresh your image and give you the confidence you need. No more worrying if your haircut will be good — know you are getting a great service.
